Common Tax Deductions for Resellers

Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)

This is the #1 deduction. It's the original price you paid for the items you sold. If you bought a video game for $5 and sold it for $40, your COGS is $5.

Shipping Costs

You can deduct the full cost of postage and shipping fees you pay to send items to buyers.

Business Mileage

Track the miles you drive for your business, such as trips to the post office or to source inventory. Use the Mileage Tracker in this app! The IRS sets a standard rate per mile each year (currently $0.67/mile for 2024).

Supplies

Boxes, bubble wrap, packing tape, printer ink, and labels are all deductible business expenses.

Platform Fees

eBay fees, PayPal fees, Mercari fees, and any other selling platform fees are deductible.

Home Office

If you use part of your home exclusively for your reselling business, you may be able to deduct a portion of your rent/mortgage, utilities, and internet.

Storage Units

If you rent a storage unit for inventory, the monthly rental cost is deductible.

Software & Apps

Subscription costs for listing tools, inventory management software, and apps like Scan2Flip Pro are deductible business expenses.
