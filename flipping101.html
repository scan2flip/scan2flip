  • Cash Conversion Cycle: The number of days from spending cash (buying an item) to getting cash back (payout). Shorter is always safer.

C. Condition & Prep

  • Test & reset electronics: Check power, buttons, and ports. Factory reset devices and remove any personal accounts or locks.
  • Accurately grade condition: New, Like-New, Used (Excellent‚ÜíGood‚ÜíFair). Always disclose any and all defects.
